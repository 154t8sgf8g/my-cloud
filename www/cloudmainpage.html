Built-in protection against malware, spam, and ransomware

When providing access to files, Disk uses encryption to ensure a high level of security. The service checks the files transferred to you and deletes those that contain malware, spam, ransomware or phishing. Disk is a cloud-based service, which means you don't have to store files locally and reduces the risk to your devices.












Applications for effective collaboration

Disk is integrated with Documents, Tables and Presentations, cloud-based applications for effective real-time collaboration.



















Integration with tools and applications your team is already working with

The disk is compatible with and complements the technologies your team uses. You don't need to convert Microsoft Office files to other formats to collaborate on them. You can edit and store more than 100 other file types, including PDF, CAD, and more.












Google Search and AI technologies for fast workflow solutions

Special tools implemented in the Disk service provide unrivaled speed, efficiency and reliability of Google search. And ready-made search filters help the team quickly find the files they need.
